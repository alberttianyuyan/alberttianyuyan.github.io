Research

Working Papers

  • From Idea to Innovation: Scientific Disclosures, Technology News, and Business Cycles [paper] [slides]

    I construct a publication-based proxy for technology news shocks. This forward-looking measure predicts delayed TFP responses and drives business cycle fluctuations. It provides a novel channel linking scientific discovery to macroeconomic expectations and real activity.

Work in Progress

  • Why Do We Disagree about the Macroeconomy? Evidence from Social Media

    I use large-scale social media data to decompose disagreement about the macroeconomy into state-based and model-based components to examine how different sources of belief disagreement arise and their potential implications for volatility and pricing in financial markets.

  • Scientific Discoveries and Predictability of Stock Returns

    I construct a novel Scientific Discovery Index (SDI) capturing early signals of scientific progress. I hypothesize that SDI predicts long-run stock returns, indicating that scientific discoveries embed forward information about future innovation, productivity, and asset values.

  • Technology News and Firm Values: How Analysts Interpret Technology News?,

    I examine how equity analysts interpret technology-related news using RavenPack event data. The project reveals how technological information is processed by financial markets, shaping investor beliefs and influencing when and how innovation becomes reflected in asset prices.
